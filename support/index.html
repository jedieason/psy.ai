Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply sign in with your Google account and start chatting with PSY AI. The app will guide you through the process of tracking your mental health and preparing for appointments.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use Firebase authentication and secure cloud storage to protect your personal information. Your conversations are encrypted and stored securely.

Can PSY AI replace my therapist or psychiatrist?

No. PSY AI is designed to complement professional mental health care, not replace it. It's a tool to help you organize your thoughts and prepare for appointments with licensed professionals.

What languages are supported?

PSY AI currently supports multiple languages including English and Traditional Chinese, with more languages coming soon.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by contacting our support team. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Privacy Policy

Crisis Resources

If you are in crisis or experiencing thoughts of suicide, please contact:

  • Emergency Services: 911 (US) or your local emergency number
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (US): 988 or 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line (US): Text HOME to 741741
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention: Find your local helpline

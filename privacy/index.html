Privacy Policy for PSYAI

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

Introduction

PSYAI ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

1. Contact Information

  • Name: Collected from Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, and medical report forms
  • Email Address: Collected from Apple Sign In and Google Sign In

2. Health Information

We collect health-related information you voluntarily provide through our medical consultation chat, including:

  • Reason for visit and medical expectations
  • Mood, sleep, and autonomic nervous system symptoms
  • Stress events affecting emotions
  • Other medical symptoms
  • Past medical history and chronic conditions
  • Living situation and family composition
  • Substance use habits (smoking, alcohol, etc.)
  • Preliminary diagnosis information

3. User Content

  • Chat Conversations: Your conversations with the AI assistant
  • Error Reports: When errors occur, we collect error messages and conversation history for debugging purposes

4. Identifiers

  • User ID: A unique identifier from Firebase Authentication used to link your medical reports to your account

5. Usage Data

  • Product Interaction: Chat conversation history to provide AI responses and maintain context

6. Diagnostic Data

  • Error Information: Error messages, timestamps, and app state at the time of errors for app stability and debugging

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide secure access to the app
  • Medical Consultation: To provide AI-powered medical consultation and maintain your medical records
  • Service Improvement: To debug errors and improve app functionality
  • Record Keeping: To store and retrieve your medical consultation records

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database: For encrypted medical record storage
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database services
  • Google Sign In: Authentication option
  • Apple Sign In: Authentication option
  • Google Cloud Functions: AI chat processing (Gemini API)
  • Google Apps Script: Error reporting

These services may have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data collection

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
